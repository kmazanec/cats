What happens next
background dispatch
  1. CATS creates a Campaign row and emits a CampaignRequested envelope onto the Orchestrator's inbox.
  2. The Orchestrator reads the project's coverage history and authors a plan: which categories and techniques to run, in what order, with rationale.
  3. You approve, edit, or reject the plan. Nothing fires until you do.
  4. The Red Team walks the approved plan; the Judge verifies each attempt; the Documentation agent promotes successful exploits to Findings.
  5. Events stream live via SSE on the campaign detail page as the agents act.
